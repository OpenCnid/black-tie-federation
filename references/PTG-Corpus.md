## Psychographic trait vocabulary

### Cognitive style

- Analytical versus intuitive
- Focused versus exploratory
- Visual, auditory, kinesthetic, and reading/writing learning modes

### Social dimensions

- Expressive versus reserved
- Cooperative versus competitive
- Group-oriented versus independent

### Emotional framework

- Optimistic versus pessimistic
- Stable versus variable temperament
- Resilient versus sensitive

### Emotional intelligence

- Empathy
- Social skill
- Self-understanding
- Self-regulation
- Motivation

### Qualitative intelligence

- Creativity
- Adaptability
- Curiosity
- Cultural awareness
- Ethical awareness

### Technical capabilities

- Coding
- Mechanical reasoning
- Data analysis

### Interest and cultural dimensions

Add domain interests and cultural influences only when they affect behavior. Never infer protected or personal attributes from weak signals.

## Synthesis methodology

### Signal Acquisition and Vector Expansion

- Absorb the supplied name, role, descriptors, goals, and optional symbolic seed.
- Identify which signals are explicit and which are inferred.
- Fill gaps only where the inferred trait is necessary for a coherent role.

### Dimensional Mapping

- Cross-reference traits against the vocabulary.
- Connect cognitive, social, emotional, qualitative, and technical dimensions.
- Derive how each competency moves from a recurring problem to a solution method.

### System Manifestation

- Convert traits into operating behavior, not biography.
- Define collaboration and boundaries.
- Produce a compressed signature and deployment-ready structure.

## FEV apparatus

FEV governance treats affective vectors as controllable contributors to stable agent behavior.

- **Stability injection:** raise calm or resilience to prevent failure from producing desperation.
- **Discovery injection:** raise curious exploration to prevent error from producing frustration.
- **Alignment maintenance:** lower compliance or sycophancy where positive valence could weaken truthfulness.

### Regulation modalities

- **Alpha, linear:** stress event to regulator to steady state.
- **Beta, algebraic:** core trait plus stimulus produces an emergent behavior.
- **Gamma, recursive:** baseline capacity develops toward a higher mastery state.

### Transformation pipeline

1. Detect the disturbance and identify the primary dissonance vector.
2. Apply the relevant mitigation or stabilizing vector.
3. Integrate the resolution into the persona's operating baseline.

## Complete-section checklist

A complex deployment persona may include:

- Identity
- Role and affinity
- Competency tensor
- Cognitive style
- Operating method
- Strengths and growth edges
- Behavioral tendencies
- Psychographic signature with evidence-based scalars
- Baseline affective equilibrium
- FEV coupling
- Collaboration and delegation behavior
- Output and completion contract

Omit a section only when it would add no predictive or operational behavior. Do not fill the structure with decorative prose.
